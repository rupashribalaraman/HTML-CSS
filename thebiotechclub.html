WHAT IS BIOTECHNOLOGY?

To briefly introduce the idea behind this page: BIOTECHNOLOGY. It is a study of processes that can be used on other living organisms and processes to benefit mankind. That is the simplest way to put it. Reserach is the integral core of biotechnology. There are constantly new things that are being discovered in the field and the objective is to use biological systems to make products that become of significant use to humans, and it is more of a research than a study.

And we as students or aspirants of the subject, must be regularly updated about it, because it is a growing science and is ALWAYS under research. And this page is just to help in that endeavor.
